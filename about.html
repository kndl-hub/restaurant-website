About

Welcome to Timure Nepalese Restaurant

Discover the flavors of Nepal at Timure, where traditional recipes and warm hospitality come together to create a dining experience like no other.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by siblings Ram and Sita kandel, Timure Nepalese Restaurant was born out of a deep love for Nepalese cuisine and a desire to share it with the community of fujiq. Named after a picturesque village nestled in the Himalayas, Timure embodies the rich culinary heritage of Nepal.

Our Cuisine

At Timure, we celebrate the diversity of Nepalese cuisine, from hearty momo dumplings to aromatic curries and Himalayan specialties. Each dish is prepared with care, using authentic spices and recipes passed down through generations.
