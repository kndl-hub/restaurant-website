menu

  • Dal Bhat

    Price: 1200 Yen

    A traditional Nepalese meal consisting of lentil soup (dal) served with steamed rice (bhat), accompanied by a variety of flavorful side dishes such as vegetable curry, pickles, and spinach. A wholesome and satisfying dish enjoyed daily by Nepalese families.

  • Pani Puri

    Price: 900 Yen

    Crispy, hollow puri shells filled with a spicy and tangy mixture of flavored water, chickpeas, potatoes, and tamarind chutney. A popular and delightful street food that bursts with refreshing flavors in every bite.

  • Momo

    Price: 1000 Yen

    Delicious steamed or fried dumplings filled with spiced meat or vegetables, served with a savory dipping sauce. A beloved Nepalese comfort food that is perfect for any occasion.
